Meet the artist:

Rebekah Howell is an artist who brings depth and emotion to life through charcoal drawings. Inspired by her faith and personal reflections, her work explores storytelling and themes of love, identity and connection. Each artwork tells a unique story. Her mission is to make art that feels personal, purposeful, and has connection with the viwer.
